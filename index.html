Key Features

Multi-Formats

Now supported HLS, Smooth, MPEG-DASH and can be encoded with H264/AVC, Advanced Audio Coding (AAC) & Dolby Digital AC-3.

High Availability

Next TV platform is distributed by design and tolerant to loss of any reasonable number of hardware nodes

Protection

DRM protection using Microsoft Playready (smooth), AES-128 encryption for HLS protocol (Verimatrix in plans), HAS url access control based on user ip, Protection from sharing HAS urls between users.

Small Storage

Next TV OTT platform is designed to keep storage as small as possible so there is no duplication of data for multiple formats, DRMs and device dialects.

Playback Features

Timeshift, Archive, Rewind, Pause live tv (currently smooth only), Multiple audio tracks, 5.1 audio

CDN integration

Level3, Akamai, Cdnvideo, Ngenix, Custom or private CDN could be added on demand.

Billing

Next TV provides simple REST API for HAS streams generation and statistics.

Deploy with Docker

Next TV platform is easily deployable to your private or public cloud infrastructure because we use docker as packaging and distribution tool.

Why ISP needs OTT?

Attract Customers

Customers choose ISP in competing ISP market based on provided value added services and modern multicscreen TV is a good one.

Lower your costs

Having your customers using internal video services instead of global one descrease your expenses on inbound traffic.

IPTV is outdated

IPTV or cable TV provide just linear watch experience. Your customers need broader one like archive, rewind, personalised stream. Modern non-linear TV will replace old plain IPTV/cable TV in a while.

Why choose Next TV?

Managed Solution

We provide self-hosted solution so you won’t depend on external components - everything is under your control.

Use what you already have

You probably already have content like channels for IPTV/cable TV, there is no reason to pay twice for it like you do with external “classic” OTT integrators.

Save you money on traffic

Whole infrastructure located in last mile (your trusted network) and you don’t have to worry about quality of streaming and huge incoming bandwidth used by TV service.
